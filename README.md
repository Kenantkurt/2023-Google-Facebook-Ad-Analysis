# 2023-Google-Facebook-Ad-Analysis

This repository contains the 2023 Google & Facebook advertising performance analysis, including key metrics such as spend, clicks, conversions, and ROAS.

In 2023, I analyzed the advertising performance of Google and Facebook:

**Google:**
- Total Spend: $625K
- Clicks: 418K
- ROAS: 2
- Conversions: 7,763

**Facebook:**
- Total Spend: $514K
- Clicks: 526K
- ROAS: 2.98
- Conversions: 9,895

During the analysis, I encountered challenges such as data inconsistencies and missing records, but by applying proper filtering and analytical methods, I was able to derive clear and meaningful insights.

**Key Takeaways:**
- Facebook stands out as a more efficient platform with lower costs and higher ROAS.
- Google continues to provide significant revenue with higher conversion value.

This analysis highlights how data quality and accurate analytical approaches directly impact decision-making when working with large datasets.
